Sources & Methodology

Listings emphasize museums that publicly acknowledge Chinese holdings taken during the Anglo-French looting of the Yuanmingyuan (Old Summer Palace), the Eight-Nation Alliance campaign, and related punitive expeditions. Source material includes collection catalogues, internal provenance statements, restitution reports, and investigative journalism.

Provenance investigations are ongoing. Presence in this list does not necessarily mean every Chinese object in the collection was taken illicitly; instead, it reflects institutional records documenting at least part of the holdings as seized or distributed from military expeditions.
